A/1200+

Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) Baggers

The All-Fill A/1200+ Vertical Form Fill & Seal Bagger can provide accurate, efficient packaging of pillow, gusseted, Doy-Style standup bags and hole-punches as well. It is capable of forming and filling bags measuring 2.5” x 3” to 8” x 14” with volumes ranging from .5 oz to 5 lb, at speeds up to 100 cycles per minute with a servo jaw closure. Supported laminate film and unsupported polyethylene stocks can be easily handled with film thickness up to 0.006.” Features include 7-inch widescreen color HMI, 50 stored routines, and tool-less changeovers. The Model A/1200+ is directly compatible with all filling and checkweighing equipment from All-Fill. Color touch screen controls provide easy setup, testing, and operation from self-explanatory screens and selections. Multiple recipes stored in memory can be used for each product, filling amount, or type. As with all our machines, equipment manufacturing, and software programming are performed in-house for continuous, assured service, support, and assistance.

Specifications

  • Package Sizes
    2.5” x 3” to 8” x 14”
  • Package Weights
    Forms and fills 0.5 oz. to 5 lbs.
  • Production Rates
    Up to 100 cycles per minute
  • Tool Size Changeover
    Tool-less changeover
  • Machine Dimensions
    46.5” x 72.75” x 57”
  • Roll Diameter
    14”
  • Electrical
    208/240V, Single Phase, 50/60 HZ., 25A (other voltages available)
  • Pneumatic
    Up to 15 SCFM at 70 PSI
  • Component Types
    Common, off-the-shelf components
  • Routine Storage
    PLC control stores 50 routines with a 7” widescreen color HMI
  • Environment
    50°F (10°C) to 90°F (32°C) up to 95% relative humidity

All dimensions and specifications are subject to change without notice. Consult your sales representative or email us at info@allfill.com for exact information prior to purchase.
