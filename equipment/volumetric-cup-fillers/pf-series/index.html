PF/Series

Fillers

The PF/Series cup filler is designed to fill uniformed products such as paint balls, pretzel nuggets, and candy by count. Individual pockets for each item are machined into a delrin plate and indexed via a servo motor for the correct count. Fill counts as low as 1 can be achieved.

Get a quote

Specifications

  • Construction
    Painted aluminum base, anodized aluminum, stainless steel contact parts, stainless construction is available
  • Production Rates
    Speeds ranging up to 120 CPM, depending on amount of pockets
  • Controls
    Embedded controller, 10.00" color touch screen HMI, foot pedal initiation
  • Control Features
    Individual control of all settings, alphanumeric product naming, containers per minute readout, Users / Maintenance / Supervisor Login, statistical analysis of a shift run
  • Hopper Capacity
    Supply hopper capacity of 1.5 cu. ft., other options available: hopper extensions and covers
  • Machine Dimensions
    Custom
  • Storage
    100 product memory
  • Electrical Requirements
    220V/1ph/60HZ (standard other voltages available)
  • Pneumatic Requirements
    2 SCFM @ 60 PSI
  • Changeovers
    Fix mounted - tools required
  • Options
    Vibratory pan infeed. Product level sensor. Single or dual discharge. Servo cup position control. Conveyor with indexing/automation may require container lift. Nylon coating and dimpled contact surfaces. Statistical package (USB). Various washdown and sanitary designs. Variety of fill accessories. Nozzles or chutes. Product level control to signal infeed device. Hopper cover. Integration with OEM. 316 stainless steel contact and quick removal parts available.

All dimensions and specifications are subject to change without notice. Consult your sales representative or email us at info@allfill.com for exact information prior to purchase.
